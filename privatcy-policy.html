1. Introduction

Welcome to LIKApps ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This comprehensive Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our platform and all web applications developed under the LIKApps ecosystem, including but not limited to applications starting with the "LIKApps" prefix (e.g., "LIKAppsShare," "LIKAppsManager," "LIKAppsAnalyzer," etc.).

This policy applies to all services, websites, and applications operated under the LIKApps brand, regardless of the specific application name or functionality. By accessing or using any LIKApps application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • "LIKApps Platform" refers to the entire ecosystem of web applications developed and maintained by LIKApps, including all applications prefixed with "LIKApps".
  • "Personal Information" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • "Services" refers collectively to all web applications, websites, and services provided under the LIKApps brand.
  • "User" means any individual who accesses or uses any LIKApps application.
  • "Google OAuth 2.0" refers to the authentication protocol provided by Google LLC that allows users to log in to our applications using their Google account credentials.
